Hello! This is **solved.ac**, the community powered guide for programming challenges.

On May 22, 2025, due to a BOJ server issue, functions such as loging in and submitting code were not working properly.
As a result, solved.ac has granted a **Streak Repair** item to all users to restore their streak for May 22, 2025.

After logging in to solved.ac, you can use the item in your [inventory](https://solved.ac/inventory).
